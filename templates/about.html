About SBITM

Established with a vision to bring world-class technical education to Betul.

Our Vision

To be a premier institute of technical education, fostering innovation, leadership, and ethical values in students to serve the society and nation.

Our Mission

  • Provide value-based technical education.
  • Create an ecosystem for research and innovation.
  • Foster industry linkages for better employability.

Our Legacy

Shri Balaji Institute of Technology and Management (SBITM), Betul, established in 2009, has emerged as a beacon of excellence in Central India. Founded under the Shri Balaji Shiksha Samiti, the institute sits on a sprawling 20-acre lush green campus.

We offer AICTE approved undergraduate programs in Computer Science, Civil, Mechanical, and Electrical Engineering, affiliated to RGPV, Bhopal.

Our Legacy & Heritage

Established in 2009, Shri Balaji Institute of Technology and Management (SBITM), Betul, has emerged as a beacon of excellence in technical education in Central India. Founded under the aegis of the Shri Balaji Shiksha Samiti, the institute was conceived with a noble vision to bring world-class engineering and management education to the doorstep of students in the region.

Over the last decade, SBITM has evolved from a budding college into a premier integrated campus. We take pride in our sprawling 20-acre lush green campus that provides a serene and stimulating environment for intellectual growth. Our journey has been marked by continuous innovation in teaching pedagogies, strengthening of industry-academia interface, and a relentless commitment to holistic student development.

We offer undergraduate programs in Computer Science, Civil, Mechanical, Electronics & Communication, and Electrical Engineering, alongside a robust MBA program. All our courses are approved by AICTE, New Delhi, and affiliated to RGPV, Bhopal and DAVV, Indore.
